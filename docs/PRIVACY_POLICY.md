## How we use it

- **Provide the service.** Accounts, workspaces, projects — you gave
  us this data so we could show you your own workspace.
- **Security + abuse prevention.** Rate limits, audit log, anomaly
  detection. Legal basis: legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)).
- **Compliance.** Keep records regulators require us to keep. Legal
  basis: legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)).
- **Support.** If you contact us, we use your email and message.
- **Communication.** Service notices (mandatory) and opt-in marketing
  (you can unsubscribe any time).

We do **not** sell your personal data. We do not use it to train
third-party AI models.

## Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:

- **Access** your data (Art. 15) — self-serve via Settings →
  Download my data
- **Rectify** inaccurate data (Art. 16) — update via Settings
- **Erase** your data (Art. 17) — Settings → Delete my account
- **Restrict** processing (Art. 18) — email us
- **Port** your data (Art. 20) — same self-serve export above
- **Object** to processing (Art. 21) — email us
- **Not be subject to solely automated decisions** (Art. 22) — we
  don't do this

To exercise any right, email `[privacy@example.com]` or use the
self-serve Settings page. We respond within **30 days** (Art. 12(3)).

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory
authority — in the UK that's the ICO (`ico.org.uk`).
